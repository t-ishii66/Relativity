# AUTHORING_GUIDELINES

## Basic Workflow

- Author the source manuscript in Japanese in `Relativity.md`.
- After the Japanese manuscript is updated, produce `Relativity-en.md` by translating it into English.

## Translation Policy

- Do not summarize.
- Aim for one-to-one correspondence between Japanese and English content.
- Translate as accurately as possible while preserving nuance and tone.

## Image Rule

- In the English edition, replace `images/image11.png` with `images/image11-en.png`.

## Intentional Omission in English Edition

- Remove the following two-line explanation from the English edition:

```text
- Projectile path is a misconception: 放物軌道は錯覚
- Actually a constant velocity path: 本当は等速直線運動
```
